who's this guy?

The short version: curious about everything, building to learn.

photo

I'm Kaden Priebe, a student at Cornell studying at the intersection of biology and computation. By day I'm in the lab working on cancer research ‚Äî by night I'm building side projects, making YouTube videos, and probably debugging something that should've worked the first time.

I got into research through a genuine curiosity about how cells make decisions ‚Äî how a tumor cell "decides" to metastasize, how gene regulatory networks coordinate. That same curiosity drives everything else I do: building tools, writing code, making videos about what I'm learning.

I believe the best way to learn something is to build something with it. This site is proof of that philosophy (and also my excuse to procrastinate on problem sets).
